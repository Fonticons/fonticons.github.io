Peas in a Pod

@supercodepoet wrote this on 29 Jul 2015 Tweet

Brothers don't shake hands. Brothers gotta HUG!

We love to enjoy the great beauty of this world. An exquisite single origin coffee, roasted to perfection and lovingly brewed with pour over. That pleasant surprise when you get the exact food pairing for the wine you are drinking. The satisfaction of getting just the right icons and font to make your website pop.

Just like wine and food work together we believe typography fonts and icon sets are like two peas in a pod. We felt so strongly about this we added a feature to Fonticons allowing you to add typography fonts to your kit!

Serve Fonts with Your Kits!

We have added the ability to upload fonts that you already own or pick from 5 beautiful fonts we have hand selected: Arvo, Lato, Merriweather, Open Sans, and Raleway. These fonts can be added to your kit and served up just like your icons. We think this will make super simple to get just the right pairing of fonts and icons, use them in your site and serve them up blazing fast from a CDN.

We are so excited for you to try it out we have added this feature to all our plans, including free! We can’t wait to see what pairings you guys come up with. We will be working on some of our own pairings and release our version of tasting notes :)

Again, if the is anything you would like to see added to Fonticons or and help you are needing we are all ears. We would LOVE to hear from you hello@fonticons.com!

Simplify, Simplify.

@davegandy wrote this on 25 Jun 2015 Tweet

“Our life is frittered away by detail. Simplify, simplify.”

We recently made things simpler on Fonticons. First, we changed plans and pricing:

  • Clarified what each plan gets.
  • All paid plans now get the full Starter Library.
  • Changed to a simpler yearly billing option.

We also simplified the Kit interaction. This should be especially helpful for new users:

  • Removed all the confusing steps.
  • No longer need to add an icon set before you can start adding icons.
  • All important actions are now right on the Kit page — settings, publish kit, edit icon, etc.
  • New Kits can start with the 20 most popular Font Awesome icons or they can start empty.

We're confident this is the right mental model for interacting with Kits. But we know we have a lot of work left to speed things up for our serious users, so stay tuned for improvements when adding multiple icons. We hear you, and we're working on it!

Stay, Unicode, Stay. Good Unicode.

@supercodepoet wrote this on 12 May 2015 Tweet

“You said stay so I stayed."

One of the best things about my job is getting to deliver a feature our awesome users have asked for. Today, I am happy to announce changes to how unicode values are handled in your kits!

Before when you added icons to a kit we waited until you published the kit to assign a unicode value to it. The problem was that when you removed an earlier icon all the unicode values could get shuffled when publishing the kit again.

Now when you add an icon to a kit we generate its unicode value right then. You have the ability to change this value on the "Tweak Kit" tab of your kit's settings. You can also see these values in the kit's documentation found under the "Publish Kit" tab.

With this change we hope to make the transition from regular Font Awesome to using Fonticons much easier. We also hope that this will allow our users to do some new and amazing things in their CSS with their kits.

We try very hard to make Fonticons the best it can be. We're always listening to new suggestions, so keep'em coming!
