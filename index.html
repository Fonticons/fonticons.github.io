Introducing Fonticons

@davegandy wrote this on 23 Oct 2014 Tweet

After several months of hard work, Travis and I are crazy excited to announce a limited release of the Fonticons beta. Interested? Head on over to request an invitation and we'll get you one as soon as we can.

Fonticons makes web icons fast and easy.

With Fonticons, you'll be able to choose just the right icon set to match the look and feel of your website and get just the icons and CSS you need — making your site load faster.

Icon Fonts

“I think this is the beginning of a beautiful friendship.”

Icon fonts are a great way to get scalable vector graphics on your website and have complete control over them with CSS. That's why I created Font Awesome a couple of years ago. Font Awesome gives you a large set of scalable vector icons for just about anything your site needs. Font Awesome's CSS features make it even easier to use icons at larger sizes, as bullets, or even spin, stack, and rotate icons.

According to meanpath, Font Awesome is now on almost 3 million websites. And we continue to see more and more traffic every month.

Why Fonticons?

“I'm your huckleberry.”

As simple and powerful as icon fonts are to use, they aren't perfect.

First, people visiting your website have to download every single icon in the set. Our research shows that most websites only use around 20 icons total. Font Awesome, for example, has almost 500 icons. That's a lot of icons going across the wire that your site isn't using. And there's likely a good bit of CSS you don't need either. That slows your site down.

Second, what if your icon set doesn't have the exact icon you need? Or what if you want to serve up your company logo? Creating your own icon font is no simple task. You'll need to learn a typesetting tool, figure out how font metrics work, make your icons pixel perfect, export to the right webfont formats, figure out the CSS to integrate everything, then get your web server to serve up the custom font to every browser without cross-site failures. It's a big bag of hurt.

Web icons should easier. That's why we made Fonticons.

Tons of Icon Sets

“We need to talk about your flair.”

Just like Helvetica isn't the perfect typographic font for every website on earth, one single icon set isn't perfect for everyone either. The right set of icons reinforces the exact look and feel you need. We believe iconography should have the same level of variety, beauty, and refinement seen in traditional typography.

To give you the best icon sets to choose from, we've scoured the web and heavily curated just the right ones. We've made sure each set has a large number of icons, so you'll be more likely to find the perfect icon when you need it.

Curious? Check out our icon sets »

We're carefully adding new icon sets as we find just the right ones. Think we're missing a set? Let me know.

Speed up Your Website

“Faster! Faster! Faster would be better!”

Since Fonticons allows you to load just the icons and CSS your website uses, you'll drastically speed up their load time.

For example, if this blog were using stock Font Awesome 4.2.0, our icons and CSS would weigh in at 87.4kb. Using Fonticons, this blog's kit weighs in at 4.06kb. That's a 95% bandwidth savings.

Powerful, Simple CSS Toolkit

“I'm giving her all she's got, Captain!”

We've paired the best icon sets with Font Awesome's powerful CSS toolkit. That makes it easier to control and style your icons exactly the way you need. You can also pare down to just the CSS your site needs, making your kit load even faster.

“She'll make it point-five past lightspeed.”

We've partnered up with MaxCDN to serve your icons crazy fast. Their global network means your kit loads faster and more reliably anywhere in the world. Even our free accounts are served by MaxCDN!
