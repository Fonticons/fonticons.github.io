Changes and Updates

@davegandy wrote this on 29 Jan 2015 Tweet

Invites, Invites, Invites

Yesterday we sent out several thousand invites to many of you that have been waiting. The response has been overwhelming and we're adding capacity to handle the increased traffic! Thanks for being patient as we iron things out a bit. :)

Changes to Plans

Today, we added the Blogger plan at $4 a month. This plan is designed to meet the needs of some of our more casual users that also want access to lots of great icon sets and more custom icons.

During beta testing, we included an option for folks to be able to use the service for free. This has been a huge help for us to work out the kinks and get some great feedback.

Next week, we'll be removing the ability for new users to sign up for the free account. We'll continue supporting the free plans for everyone who signed up early as a thank you for helping us out early on. So THANK YOU!

If you're looking for a great free option for using Font Awesome, BootstrapCDN is fantastic. As always, thanks to the venerable MaxCDN for hosting!

Black Tie coming in February!

I'm putting the finishing touches on Black Tie, the multi-weight, commercial icon font I Kickstarted last year. As soon as it's ready, we'll be adding this icon set to all paid accounts in February.

Changes Based on Feedback

Based on great feedback from our early users, we're working on making a few changes:

  • No credit card required for free trial
  • Yearly billing options
  • Options for kit download
  • Commercial icon sets! We're working on a few deals to bring some absolutely amazing icon sets into Fonticons. We'll keep you posted with any news!
